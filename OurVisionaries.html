Our Visionaries

The hearts and minds behind the Vagan movement. We're on a mission to make plant-based living accessible, delicious, and undeniably sustainable for everyone.

The Visionary

Alex Rivers

Chief Green Officer

Alex is a culinary innovator with 15 years of experience in plant-based nutrition. Her journey started in the heart of San Francisco, where she realized the disconnect between traditional gourmet dining and ethical consumption.

Her passion lies in creating flavors that bridge the gap between tradition and sustainability. At Vagan, she oversees our recipe development and flavor science lab, ensuring that "vegan" never means "compromise."

"We aren't just changing plates; we're changing perspectives on what food can be."

The Strategist

Jordan Lee

Head of Sustainability

Jordan's background in environmental science drives Vagan's commitment to ethical sourcing and zero-waste logistics. He spent a decade advising global logistics firms on carbon neutrality before deciding to build something of his own.

He believes the future is green or not at all. Under his leadership, Vagan has achieved 100% plastic-free packaging and established direct-trade relationships with over 200 sustainable farms worldwide.

"Sustainability isn't a feature; it's our foundation. Every choice we make considers the seventh generation."
